BRIIISK Mercantile Pvt Ltd is a company with a vision to simplify the various day to day life activities like booking tickets to banking, recharging to billing, etc by providing various services ranging from basic Govt based services to hospitality, IT, e-commerce, Banking, all your needs under one roof.

The mission is led by the directors of the organization Mr. Syed Imran and Mrs. Afreen Abdulrub Shaikh who believe in the ideology that “Alone one does little and Together we can do so much”. And hence to see their vision come true they are providing you the opportunity to be their partner at a village level or tehsil or district or state level and realize your dream of becoming an entrepreneur.
